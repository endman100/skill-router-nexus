Our Process

From Seed to Cup

01

Source

We visit farms during harvest. We cup hundreds of lots. We choose the ones that tell the clearest story of their soil and altitude.

02

Roast

Small-batch roasting on a Loring S15 Falcon. Each lot gets its own profile — developed over weeks of cupping and dialing in the curve.

03

Rest

Fresh-roasted coffee needs 5–7 days to degas. We time our shipping so beans arrive in your grinder at peak flavor.

04

Brew

Every bag includes a brew guide with our recommended ratio, grind size, and water temperature. Dial in once, enjoy for weeks.
